# Customer Conflict Resolution - Audio Script
## Resolución de Conflictos con Clientes

**Duration**: 9 minutes | **Level**: Emergency | **Category**: All Workers

---

## CRITICAL WARNING

Este audio te enseña cómo desescalar conflictos y proteger tu seguridad cuando clientes se vuelven difíciles o agresivos.

Puedes terminar CUALQUIER viaje si te sientes inseguro. Tu seguridad es más importante que ratings o dinero.

---

## Phrase 1: Show Empathy First

### English:
"I understand your concern"
"I understand your concern"

### Spanish:
Entiendo su preocupación.

LA frase más poderosa para desescalar. Empieza SIEMPRE con empatía.

---

## Phrase 2: Offer to Help

### English:
"Let me help you resolve this"
"Let me help you resolve this"

### Spanish:
Déjeme ayudarle a resolver esto.

Después de empatía, inmediatamente ofrece solución.

---

## Phrase 3: Request Respect

### English:
"I need us to communicate respectfully"
"I need us to communicate respectfully"

### Spanish:
Necesito que nos comuniquemos respetuosamente.

Si cliente usa lenguaje abusivo. Firme pero educado.

---

## Phrase 4: Direct to Platform

### English:
"You can dispute this through the app support"
"You can dispute this through the app support"

### Spanish:
Puede disputar esto a través del soporte de la aplicación.

Para disputas de tarifa o servicio fuera de tu control.

---

## Phrase 5: Final Warning

### English:
"If you cannot be respectful, I will end this trip"
"If you cannot be respectful, I will end this trip"

### Spanish:
Si no puede ser respetuoso, terminaré este viaje.

Última advertencia antes de terminar servicio.

---

## Handling Fare Disputes

### English:
"The price is set by the app based on distance, time, and demand. I cannot change it."
"The price is set by the app based on distance, time, and demand. I cannot change it."

### Spanish:
El precio lo establece la aplicación según distancia, tiempo y demanda. No puedo cambiarlo.

---

## Route Complaints

### English:

"I followed the app's navigation to avoid traffic. Here's the route we took."

### Spanish:
Seguí la navegación de la app para evitar tráfico. Aquí está la ruta que tomamos.

"I need us to communicate respectfully. I'm here to help."

### Spanish:

## Personal Attack - Second Warning

### English:

"If you cannot be respectful, I will have to end this trip."

### Spanish:

---

## Ending Trip for Safety

### English:
"I'm ending this trip now. Please exit the vehicle."
"I'm ending this trip now. Please exit the vehicle."

### Spanish:
Estoy terminando este viaje ahora. Por favor salga del vehículo.

Firm, authoritative tone.

---

## Key Emergency Phrases

1. "I understand your concern"
2. "Let me help you resolve this"
3. "I need us to communicate respectfully"
4. "You can dispute through app support"
5. "I'm ending this trip now"

---

## REMEMBER

1. Empathy desescalates most conflicts
2. Never tolerate abuse - you have right to end trips
3. Document everything immediately
4. Report to platform before customer does
5. Your safety is more important than any rating

---

**Voice Notes**:
- Calm, de-escalating tone
- English: Firm but professional
- Practice with assertive confidence
- Safety-first messaging

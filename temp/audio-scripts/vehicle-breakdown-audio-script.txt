# Vehicle Breakdown Emergency - Audio Script
## Avería del Vehículo

**Duration**: 8 minutes | **Level**: Emergency | **Category**: All Workers

---

## CRITICAL INFO

Vehicle problems don't penalize you if reported correctly. How you handle it affects your rating more than the problem itself.

---

## Phrase 1: Report Breakdown

### English:
"My vehicle has broken down"
"My vehicle has broken down"

### Spanish:
Mi vehículo se ha averiado.

Use IMMEDIATELY when discovering serious problem.

---

## Phrase 2: Need to Pull Over

### English:
"I need to pull over immediately"
"I need to pull over immediately"

### Spanish:
Necesito detenerme inmediatamente.

Warn passenger of emergency stop.

---

## Phrase 3: Call Roadside Assistance

### English:
"I'm calling roadside assistance"
"I'm calling roadside assistance"

### Spanish:
Estoy llamando a asistencia en carretera.

Explain your next action to passenger.

---

## Phrase 4: Cannot Continue

### English:
"I apologize, but I cannot continue this trip"
"I apologize, but I cannot continue this trip"

### Spanish:
Disculpe, pero no puedo continuar este viaje.

Professional and empathetic.

---

## Phrase 5: Customer Won't Be Charged

### English:
"You won't be charged for this trip"
"You won't be charged for this trip"

### Spanish:
No se le cobrará por este viaje.

Reassures anxious customer.

---

## Common Problems

Flat tire = Llanta ponchada
Dead battery = Batería muerta
Overheating = Sobrecalentamiento
Out of gas = Sin gasolina
Won't start = No arranca

---

## Calling Roadside Assistance

### English:
"I need roadside assistance. I'm a rideshare driver at [location]."
"I need roadside assistance. I'm a rideshare driver at [location]."

### Spanish:
Necesito asistencia en carretera. Soy conductor de rideshare en [ubicación].

---

## Help Customer Get Another Ride

### English:
"The platform will send another driver at no charge to you."
"The platform will send another driver at no charge to you."

### Spanish:
La plataforma enviará otro conductor sin cargo para usted.

---

**Voice Notes**:
- Professional, problem-solving tone

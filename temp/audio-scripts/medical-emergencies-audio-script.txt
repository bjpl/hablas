# Medical Emergency - Audio Script
## Emergencias Médicas

**Duration**: 10 minutes | **Level**: CRITICAL | **Category**: All Workers

---

## LIFE-SAVING INFORMATION

This audio contains phrases that can SAVE LIVES. Practice now to be prepared.

Calling 911 is ALWAYS FREE - even from phones without service.

---

## FIRST: Call 911 Immediately

### English:
"Call 911 immediately!"
"Call 911 immediately!"

### Spanish:
¡Llame al 911 inmediatamente!

THE most important phrase. ANY medical emergency - call 911 FIRST.

---

## SECOND: Check Consciousness

### English:
"Are you okay? Can you hear me?"
"Are you okay? Can you hear me?"

### Spanish:
¿Está bien? ¿Puede oírme?

Verify if person is conscious.

---

## THIRD: State Emergency

### English:
"I need medical help now"
"I need medical help now"

### Spanish:
Necesito ayuda médica ahora.

What you say to 911 operator.

---

## FOURTH: Keep Calm

### English:
"Stay calm, help is on the way"
"Stay calm, help is on the way"

### Spanish:
Mantenga la calma, la ayuda viene en camino.

Reassure the person in emergency.

---

## FIFTH: Don't Move Them

### English:
"Don't move, wait for the paramedics"
"Don't move, wait for the paramedics"

### Spanish:
No se mueva, espere a los paramédicos.

CRITICAL: Moving injured person can cause permanent damage.

---

## CALLING 911: Give Location

"911, what's your emergency?"

### English:
"My passenger is having a medical emergency. We're at Main Street and Oak Avenue."
"My passenger is having a medical emergency. We're at Main Street and Oak Avenue."

### Spanish:
Mi pasajero está teniendo una emergencia médica. Estamos en Main Street y Oak Avenue.

---

## CALLING 911: Describe Condition

Heart attack symptoms:
"The person has chest pain, is sweating, and having trouble breathing."

Stroke symptoms:
"The person's face is drooping and they can't speak clearly."

Seizure:
"The person is having a seizure. They're shaking uncontrollably."

Unconscious:
"The person is unconscious and not responding to me."

---

## ESSENTIAL VOCABULARY

Emergency = Emergencia
Ambulance = Ambulancia
Unconscious = Inconsciente
Chest pain = Dolor de pecho
Breathing = Respirando
Seizure = Convulsión
Bleeding = Sangrando
Paramedics = Paramédicos

## REMEMBER FOREVER

1. ALWAYS call 911 first - don't drive to hospital
2. 911 is FREE - even from phones without service
3. Keep person still until help arrives
4. Stay with person until paramedics arrive
5. Notify platform immediately after calling 911
6. You're protected by Good Samaritan laws when helping

---

**Voice Notes**:
- SERIOUS, life-saving tone
- SLOW pace (60% speed)
- Clear, authoritative
- Emphasize ALL critical actions
- Repeat emergency procedures
- This can SAVE LIVES

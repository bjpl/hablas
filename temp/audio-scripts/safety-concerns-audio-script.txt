# Personal Safety Threats - Audio Script
## Seguridad Personal - Respuesta a Amenazas

**Duration**: 9 minutes | **Level**: CRITICAL | **Category**: All Workers

---

## YOUR SAFETY FIRST

You can end ANY trip if you feel unsafe - your safety is MORE important than ratings or money.

Police take threats against workers very seriously. NEVER try to be hero - escape and report.

---

## Phrase 1: Calling Police Warning

### English:
"I'm calling the police"
"I'm calling the police"

### Spanish:
Voy a llamar a la policía.

Warning when someone threatens you. Say it firmly and DO IT if needed.

---

## Phrase 2: Get Out Now

### English:
"Get out of the car now"
"Get out of the car now"

### Spanish:
Salga del carro ahora.

To end trip IMMEDIATELY for safety. Firm, authoritative tone.

---

## Phrase 3: I Need Help

### English:
"I need help, I'm in danger"
"I need help, I'm in danger"

### Spanish:
Necesito ayuda, estoy en peligro.

When calling 911 or asking for emergency help.

---

## Phrase 4: Stay Back

### English:
"Stay back / Don't come closer"
"Stay back / Don't come closer"

### Spanish:
Manténgase atrás / No se acerque.

Create safety distance. If they continue approaching, escape.

---

## Phrase 5: Leave Me Alone

### English:
"Leave me alone"
"Leave me alone"

### Spanish:
Déjeme en paz.

Clear rejection of unwanted contact. Last phrase before asking help.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP: Threat Situation

Step 1 - Try to calm:
"I need you to calm down."

Step 2 - Set boundary:
"I will end this trip if you continue."

Step 3 - Pull over safely:
"I'm pulling over now."

Step 4 - End trip:
"This trip is over. You need to exit the vehicle."

Step 5 - Call help if needed:
"I'm calling the police now."

---

## Intoxicated & Aggressive

Warning:
"I'm getting you home safely. Please remain calm and seated."

If threatening:
"I'm pulling over. You need to calm down or exit the vehicle."

---

## Weapon Present - YOUR LIFE FIRST

DO NOT RESIST:
"Okay, okay, take whatever you want."
"I won't resist. Please don't hurt me."

After safe - call 911:
"I was just robbed at gunpoint at [location]. The suspect went [direction]."

---

## Suspicious Request

Refuse firmly:
"I'm not comfortable with that location."
"I can only go to addresses that are in the app."

---

## Being Followed

If you suspect following:
- DON'T go home
- Drive to police station
- Call police:
"I'm being followed. I need police assistance."

---

## Emergency App Button

What it does:
- Calls 911 automatically
- Shares your GPS location
- Alerts platform

When to use:
- Immediate threat
- Physical aggression
- Weapon present
- ANY situation needing police NOW

2. "I won't resist"
3. "Please don't hurt me"

Your life is worth more than ANYTHING. COMPLY. Report after safe.

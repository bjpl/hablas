# Severe Weather Safety - Audio Script
## Clima Severo - Seguridad en Condiciones Peligrosas

**Duration**: 10 minutes | **Level**: CRITICAL | **Category**: All Workers

---

## LIFE-THREATENING INFO

US weather includes tornadoes, blizzards, and hurricanes that DON'T exist in Colombia.

Weather alerts are VERY serious - follow them ALWAYS.

Platform supports ALL safety decisions related to weather. Your life is worth more than any trip or rating.

---

## TORNADO ALERT - ACT IMMEDIATELY

Step 1 - Recognize:
"There's a tornado warning for this area."

Step 2 - Find shelter:
"Follow me to shelter. We need to go inside now."

Step 3 - Explain danger:
"Tornadoes are extremely dangerous. We cannot stay in the vehicle."

CRITICAL: Tornado WARNING means confirmed tornado - act NOW.

---

## TORNADO INFO (Doesn't exist in Colombia)

What is it:
"A tornado is a violent rotating windstorm. They're very common here and extremely deadly."

Golden rule:
"NEVER try to outrun a tornado in your vehicle. Take shelter immediately."

Where to shelter:
- Inside substantial building
- Lowest floor, interior room
- Away from windows

---

## FLASH FLOOD

Critical rule:
"Turn around, don't drown. NEVER drive through flooded roads."

To passenger:
"The road ahead is flooded. For safety, I must take an alternate route or end the trip here."

Important fact:
"Six inches of water can sweep away most vehicles. You cannot judge the depth by looking."

---

## ICE / BLACK ICE

Warn passenger:
"Road conditions are icy. I need to drive much slower than normal for everyone's safety."

If too dangerous:
"The roads are too icy to continue safely. I need to end this trip."

Explain black ice:
"Black ice is invisible ice on the road. It's extremely slippery and dangerous."

---

## HAIL STORM

Pull under cover:
"I'm pulling under cover. Hail can break windows and total the vehicle."

Seek:
- Parking garage
- Gas station canopy
- Overpass (only for hail, NOT tornado)

---

## EXTREME HEAT

If someone shows heat stroke symptoms:
"You need air conditioning and water immediately. I'm taking you somewhere cool."

Symptoms:
"If you feel dizzy, nauseous, or stop sweating in extreme heat, that's an emergency."

---

## WEATHER ALERTS

Recommend downloading:
"I recommend downloading a weather alert app. Weather here can be dangerous."

Important apps:
- NOAA Weather Radio
- Weather Channel app
- Local news apps

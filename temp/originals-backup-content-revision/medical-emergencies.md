## Medical Conditions Reference

### Heart Attack

**Symptoms (English):** Chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea, cold sweat

**Síntomas (Español):** Dolor de pecho, falta de aire, náusea, sudor frío

**Action:** Call 911. Keep person still. Loosen tight clothing. If they have aspirin and can swallow, offer one

### Stroke

**Symptoms (English):** Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty, sudden confusion

**Síntomas (Español):** Cara caída, debilidad en brazo, dificultad al hablar, confusión repentina

**Action:** Call 911. Note time symptoms started. Keep person calm and still

### Diabetic

**Symptoms (English):** Confusion, shakiness, sweating, loss of consciousness

**Síntomas (Español):** Confusión, temblores, sudoración, pérdida de conocimiento

**Action:** If conscious, give sugar (juice, candy). If unconscious, call 911 immediately

### Seizure

**Symptoms (English):** Uncontrolled shaking, loss of consciousness, stiffening

**Síntomas (Español):** Temblores incontrolados, pérdida de conocimiento, rigidez

**Action:** Call 911. Clear area around person. Don't restrain. Time the seizure. Turn on side after it stops

### Allergic Reaction

**Symptoms (English):** Swelling of face/throat, difficulty breathing, hives, rapid pulse

**Síntomas (Español):** Hinchazón de cara/garganta, dificultad para respirar, urticaria, pulso rápido

**Action:** Call 911. Ask if they have EpiPen. Help them use it if needed. Keep them still

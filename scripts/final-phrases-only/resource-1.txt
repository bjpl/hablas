# Resource 1: Essential Delivery Phrases - Variation 1
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Repartidor
**Duration**: 4 minutes
**Phrases**: 13 essential phrases
**Focus**: Core delivery communication

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: ARRIVING & GREETING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Initial greeting at customer's door ===

Good morning! I have a delivery for you.

Good morning! I have a delivery for you.

Buenos días! Tengo una entrega para ti.

[Tip: Use "Good morning" (before noon), "Good afternoon" (noon-5pm), or "Good evening" (after 5pm)]


=== PHRASE 2: Confirm customer identity ===

Are you Sarah?

Are you Sarah?

¿Eres Sarah?

[Tip: Replace "Sarah" with the customer's name from the app. This prevents wrong deliveries]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: CONFIRMING LOCATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 3: Verify address ===

Is this 425 Main Street?

Is this 425 Main Street?

¿Esta es la 425 de la calle Main?

[Tip: Use when the building numbers are unclear or confusing]


=== PHRASE 4: Ask for drop-off preference ===

Where would you like me to leave it?

Where would you like me to leave it?

¿Dónde quieres que lo deje?

[Common responses: "At the door" / "Hand it to me" / "On the table"]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: HANDLING INSTRUCTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 5: Acknowledge special instructions ===

I see your note. No problem!

I see your note. No problem!

Vi tu nota. ¡Sin problema!

[Tip: This shows you read their delivery instructions and builds trust]


=== PHRASE 6: Request customer comes outside ===

Can you come outside?

Can you come outside?

¿Puedes salir afuera?

[Tip: Use when you can't access building or can't find apartment]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: FINDING THE LOCATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 7: Ask for visual identification ===

What color is your house?

What color is your house?

¿De qué color es tu casa?

[Tip: Helpful in neighborhoods with similar-looking houses]


=== PHRASE 8: Request directions ===

Can you give me directions?

Can you give me directions?

¿Me puedes dar instrucciones?

[Tip: Better than getting lost. Customers appreciate the direct communication]


=== PHRASE 9: Arrange lobby meetup ===

Can you meet me in the lobby?

Can you meet me in the lobby?

¿Puedes encontrarme en el lobby?

[Tip: Perfect for secured buildings where you can't go upstairs]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: COMPLETING DELIVERY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 10: Hand over the order ===

Here you go! Have a great day!

Here you go! Have a great day!

¡Aquí tienes! ¡Que tengas un gran día!

[Tip: A friendly tone increases tips. Smile when you say this!]


=== PHRASE 11: Thank the customer ===

Thank you! Take care!

Thank you! Take care!

¡Gracias! ¡Cuídate!

[Tip: Use when customer tips or is especially friendly]


=== PHRASE 12: Wish them well with their meal ===

Enjoy your meal!

Enjoy your meal!

¡Buen provecho!

[Cultural note: "Enjoy your meal" is common courtesy in US delivery culture]


=== PHRASE 13: Confirm contactless drop-off ===

I left it at the door for you.

I left it at the door for you.

Lo dejé en la puerta para ti.

[Tip: Say this if you need to leave before they answer, or send as text message]

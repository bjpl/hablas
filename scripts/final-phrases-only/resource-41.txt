SECTION 2: NEGOTIATION PHRASES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 6: Acknowledge while setting boundaries ===

I understand your request, but I need to follow platform policies

I understand your request, but I need to follow platform policies

Entiendo su solicitud, pero debo seguir las políticas de la plataforma

[Tip: Show empathy first, then explain limits. Customers accept "no" better this way]


=== PHRASE 7: Collaborative problem-solving ===

Let's find a solution that works for both of us

Let's find a solution that works for both of us

Busquemos una solución que funcione para ambos

[Tip: Makes customer feel like partner, not opponent. Reduces conflict]


=== PHRASE 8: Present alternatives ===

I can offer you this alternative

I can offer you this alternative

Puedo ofrecerle esta alternativa

[Tip: Always try to offer SOMETHING - turns negative into positive]


=== PHRASE 9: Clear professional boundary ===

That's outside of what I can provide

That's outside of what I can provide

Eso está fuera de lo que puedo ofrecer

[Tip: Polite but firm. Use for unreasonable requests without blaming customer]


=== PHRASE 10: Maintain standards ===

I need to maintain my service standards

I need to maintain my service standards

Necesito mantener mis estándares de servicio

[Tip: Explains why you can't comply with request that would lower quality]


=== PHRASE 11: Appreciate cooperation ===

I appreciate your flexibility

I appreciate your flexibility

Agradezco su flexibilidad

[Tip: End negotiations positively. Thank customer for understanding]

PROFESSIONAL NEGOTIATION TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ Listen fully before responding
✓ Acknowledge customer feelings first
✓ Know platform policies thoroughly
✓ Offer alternatives when possible
✓ Stay calm and professional
✓ Document unusual requests
✓ Know when to escalate to support

Remember: Good negotiation protects both parties and maintains your professional reputation.

**Category**: All (Conductor & Repartidor)

**Focus**: GPS commands and finding locations

=== PHRASE 1: Turn right ===

Turn right

Turn right

Gira a la derecha

[Tip: Most common GPS command. Practice pronunciation: "tern RITE"]


=== PHRASE 2: Continue straight ===

Go straight

Go straight

Sigue derecho/recto

[Tip: "Straight" pronounced "STREIT" not "estraight"]


=== PHRASE 3: Make U-turn ===

Make a U-turn

Make a U-turn

Haz un cambio de sentido/vuelta en U

[Tip: Also called "retorno" in some countries]


=== PHRASE 4: Distance warning ===

In 500 feet, turn right

In 500 feet, turn right

En 500 pies, gira a la derecha

[Tip: 500 feet = about 150 meters or 1.5 blocks. Prepare to turn soon]

=== PHRASE 5: Ask if going to specific street ===

Are you going to Main Street?

Are you going to Main Street?

¿Vas a Main Street?

[Tip: Use passenger's destination from app]


=== PHRASE 6: Ask about cross streets ===

Is it near the corner of First and Oak?

Is it near the corner of First and Oak?

¿Está cerca de la esquina de First y

[Tip: Cross streets help identify exact location]


=== PHRASE 7: Ask which side ===

Which side of the street?

Which side of the street?

¿De qué lado de la calle?

[Tip: Prevents wrong-side stops. Important for safety]


=== PHRASE 8: Ask about building appearance ===

What does the building look like?

What does the building look like?

¿Cómo se ve el edificio?

[Tip: Visual details help when numbers aren't visible]

=== PHRASE 9: Confirm visual contact ===

Do you see my car?

Do you see my car?

¿Ves mi carro?

[Tip: Helps passenger find you in crowded areas]


=== PHRASE 10: Request meeting point ===

Can you meet me at the entrance?

Can you meet me at the entrance?

¿Puedes encontrarme en la entrada?

[Tip: Saves time in large complexes]

=== PHRASE 11: Politely ask for directions ===

Excuse me, how do I get to Oak Street?

Excuse me, how do I get to Oak Street?

Disculpe, ¿cómo llego a Oak Street?

[Tip: Always start with "Excuse me" - shows respect]


=== PHRASE 12: Ask about distance ===

How far is it?

How far is it?

¿Qué tan lejos está?

[Tip: Helps decide if you need to drive or can walk]


=== PHRASE 13: Confirm instructions ===

So, I turn left at the light?

So, I turn left at the light?

Entonces, ¿giro a la izquierda en el

[Tip: Repeat back to confirm you understood]


=== PHRASE 14: Request guidance ===

Can you guide me?

Can you guide me?

¿Puedes guiarme?

[Tip: Use with customers when totally lost]

=== PHRASE 15: Report wrong address ===

The address in the app might be wrong

The address in the app might be wrong

La dirección en la app podría estar

[Tip: Stay polite. It's not your fault but don't blame customer]


=== PHRASE 16: Explain GPS issue ===

I lost the signal

I lost the signal

Perdí la señal

[Tip: Honest explanation. Customers understand technical issues]

Is there a gate code?

# Resource 17: Rideshare Passenger Pickup & Verification
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Conductor
**Duration**: 3 minutes
**Phrases**: 13 essential phrases
**Focus**: Safe passenger verification and initial ride setup

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: INITIAL GREETING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Morning greeting ===

Hello! Good morning!

Hello! Good morning!

¡Hola! ¡Buenos días!

[Tip: Use "Good afternoon" (12pm-5pm) or "Good evening" (after 5pm)]


=== PHRASE 2: Confirm rideshare pickup ===

Are you waiting for Uber?

Are you waiting for Uber?

¿Estás esperando un Uber?

[Tip: Replace with your platform: Lyft, DiDi, etc.]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: PASSENGER VERIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 3: Request name politely ===

Can you tell me your name, please?

Can you tell me your name, please?

¿Puedes decirme tu nombre, por favor?

[Tip: More polite than just "What's your name?"]


=== PHRASE 4: Confirm passenger name ===

Are you [Name]?

Are you [Name]?

¿Eres [Nombre]?

[Tip: ALWAYS verify name. Safety first. Wrong passenger = big problem]


=== PHRASE 5: Acknowledge correct passenger ===

Perfect! Welcome!

Perfect! Welcome!

¡Perfecto! ¡Bienvenido!

[Tip: Positive confirmation. Sets friendly tone]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: DESTINATION CONFIRMATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 6: Ask destination ===

Where are we going?

Where are we going?

¿A dónde vamos?

[Tip: Simple, direct. Use when app doesn't show or to confirm]


=== PHRASE 7: Confirm specific address ===

I have you going to [address]. Is that

I have you going to [address]. Is that

Te llevo a [dirección]. ¿Es correcto?

[Tip: Always confirm before starting trip. Catches app errors]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: GROUP PASSENGERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Check if others coming ===

Is anyone else coming?

Is anyone else coming?

¿Viene alguien más?

[Tip: Ask before starting trip. Prevents awkward returns]


=== PHRASE 9: Greet multiple passengers ===

Hello everyone! Welcome!

Hello everyone! Welcome!

¡Hola a todos! ¡Bienvenidos!

[Tip: Acknowledge whole group. Makes everyone feel welcome]


=== PHRASE 10: Confirm passenger count ===

I have three passengers, correct?

I have three passengers, correct?

Tengo tres pasajeros, ¿correcto?

[Tip: Confirms what app shows. Important for safety and payment]


=== PHRASE 11: State passenger limit ===

I can take four people maximum.

I can take four people maximum.

Puedo llevar cuatro personas máximo.

[Tip: Be polite but firm. It's the law and insurance rules]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: VERIFICATION PROBLEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 12: Check if they ordered ===

Did you order an Uber?

Did you order an Uber?

¿Pediste un Uber?

[Tip: Use when someone tries to enter but you're unsure]


=== PHRASE 13: Request app confirmation ===

Can you check your app?

Can you check your app?

¿Puedes revisar tu aplicación?

[Tip: Resolves confusion. Both check apps to match]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SAFETY REMINDERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ALWAYS verify passenger name before allowing entry.
NEVER skip verification, even if you're running late.
If passenger name doesn't match, politely decline:
"I think there might be a mix-up. Can you check your app?"

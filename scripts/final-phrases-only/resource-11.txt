# Resource 11: Rideshare Driver Essentials - Passenger Greeting & Comfort
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Conductor
**Duration**: 4 minutes
**Phrases**: 15 essential phrases
**Focus**: Professional passenger service and comfort management

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: GREETING & CONFIRMING PASSENGER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Morning greeting ===

Good morning!

Good morning!

¡Buenos días!

[Tip: Use "Good afternoon" after 12pm, "Good evening" after 5pm]


=== PHRASE 2: Welcome passenger ===

Hi! Welcome!

Hi! Welcome!

¡Hola! ¡Bienvenido!

[Tip: Smile and make eye contact. First impression matters for tips]


=== PHRASE 3: Confirm passenger name ===

Are you Sarah?

Are you Sarah?

¿Eres Sarah?

[Tip: ALWAYS confirm name before letting passenger in. Safety first]


=== PHRASE 4: Confirm rideshare pickup ===

Hi! Are you waiting for an Uber?

Hi! Are you waiting for an Uber?

¡Hola! ¿Estás esperando un Uber?

[Tip: Replace "Uber" with "Lyft" or "DiDi" as needed]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: DESTINATION & ROUTE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 5: Ask about destination ===

Where are we headed today?

Where are we headed today?

¿A dónde vamos hoy?

[Tip: Friendly way to confirm destination. Shows professionalism]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: LUGGAGE ASSISTANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 6: Offer luggage help ===

Can I help you with your bags?

Can I help you with your bags?

¿Puedo ayudarte con tus maletas?

[Tip: Offer help for luggage. Great for tips, especially airport rides]


=== PHRASE 7: Direct to trunk ===

You can put it in the trunk.

You can put it in the trunk.

Puedes ponerlo en la cajuela.

[Tip: If you don't want to handle bags, direct them politely]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: COMFORT PREFERENCES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Check temperature ===

Is the temperature okay?

Is the temperature okay?

¿Está bien la temperatura?

[Tip: Ask early in ride. Shows you care about passenger comfort]


=== PHRASE 9: Offer window option ===

Feel free to open the window.

Feel free to open the window.

Siéntete libre de abrir la ventana.

[Tip: Gives passenger control. Some prefer fresh air]


=== PHRASE 10: Ask about music ===

Is the music okay, or would you like me

Is the music okay, or would you like me

¿Está bien la música, o quieres que

[Tip: Complete phrase: "...to change it?" Shows attention to preferences]


=== PHRASE 11: Offer phone charger ===

I have a charger if you need one.

I have a charger if you need one.

Tengo un cargador si necesitas uno.

[Tip: Having chargers = better ratings. Mention at start of ride]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: PASSENGER COUNT & LIMITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 12: Ask group size ===

How many people are riding with you?

How many people are riding with you?

¿Cuántas personas vienen contigo?

[Tip: Ask before they all try to get in. Prevents awkward situations]


=== PHRASE 13: State passenger limit ===

I can only take four passengers.

I can only take four passengers.

Solo puedo llevar cuatro pasajeros.

[Tip: Be polite but firm. It's the law and app rules]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 6: ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 14: Be patient ===

Take your time.

Take your time.

Tómate tu tiempo.

[Tip: Use when passenger is loading bags or getting settled. Shows professionalism]


=== PHRASE 15: Acknowledge multiple stops ===

I see you have a stop on the way.

I see you have a stop on the way.

Veo que tienes una parada en el camino.

[Tip: Acknowledge stops early. Sets expectations for route]

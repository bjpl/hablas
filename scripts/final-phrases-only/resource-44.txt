TIME MANAGEMENT BEST PRACTICES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ ARRIVAL TIMING:
- Arrive 2-3 minutes after ETA is perfect
- Arriving too early can inconvenience customer
- Arriving late frustrates customer
- Communicate if you'll be more than 2 min late

✓ SETTING EXPECTATIONS:
- Under-promise, over-deliver
- Build in buffer time for delays
- Be realistic about traffic conditions
- Know typical delays for your area/time

✓ HANDLING TIGHT SCHEDULES:
- Ask about time constraints early
- Take fastest safe route
- Keep customer updated on progress
- Don't speed or drive recklessly
- Set boundary: "I'll get you there as quickly as safely possible"

✓ COMMUNICATING DELAYS:
- Notify as soon as you know
- Give specific reason (accident, road closure, etc.)
- Provide new ETA
- Apologize sincerely
- Offer alternative if appropriate

✓ TIME-BASED TIPS TO MAXIMIZE EARNINGS:
- Peak hours: 7-9am, 5-7pm, 10pm-2am Fri/Sat
- Know your area's busy times
- Position near high-demand zones before peak
- Airport runs: monitor flight schedules
- Events: concerts, sports, conventions

Remember: Good time management = better ratings + more trips + higher earnings. Customers value reliability above almost everything.

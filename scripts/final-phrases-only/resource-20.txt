CONFLICT DE-ESCALATION GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When facing difficult situations:

1. Stay calm - Your tone matters more than words
2. Listen first - Let passenger express concern
3. Acknowledge - "I understand your frustration"
4. Explain clearly - Use these phrases
5. Set boundaries - Be firm but polite
6. Know when to end - Your safety comes first

If situation escalates:
- Pull over safely
- Contact support
- End ride if necessary
- Report to platform

Your safety is more important than any ride or rating.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PROFESSIONAL TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ALWAYS:
- Remain professional
- Document issues in app
- Follow platform policies
- Prioritize your safety

NEVER:
- Argue with passengers
- Take insults personally
- Continue unsafe situations
- Compromise your boundaries

These advanced phrases protect you while maintaining professionalism.

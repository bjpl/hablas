**Level**: Intermedio
**Category**: Conductor
**Duration**: 4 minutes
**Phrases**: 16 conversation phrases
**Focus**: Building rapport and professional conversation with passengers

CONVERSATION TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Small talk builds ratings and tips. Remember:
- Read passenger cues: Some want quiet, others want conversation
- Keep it light: Avoid politics, religion, or controversial topics
- Listen more than talk: Show genuine interest
- Don't force it: Silence is okay too
- End positively: "Have a great day/night!"

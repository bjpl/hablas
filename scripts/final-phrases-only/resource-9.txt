# Resource 9: Quick Delivery Phrases - Essential Set
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Repartidor
**Duration**: 3 minutes
**Phrases**: 14 essential phrases
**Focus**: Fast, efficient communication for busy deliveries

=== PHRASE 1: Announce delivery ===

Hello! I have your delivery.

Hello! I have your delivery.

¡Hola! Tengo tu entrega.

[Tip: Clear and direct. Gets straight to the point]


=== PHRASE 2: Confirm name with greeting ===

Hello! Are you Maria?

Hello! Are you Maria?

¡Hola! ¿Eres María?

[Tip: Combines greeting and confirmation in one phrase for speed]


=== PHRASE 3: Quick name confirmation ===

Order for Michael?

Order for Michael?

¿Orden para Michael?

[Tip: Very fast way to confirm. Use when you're in a hurry]

=== PHRASE 4: Confirm apartment ===

Is this apartment 205?

Is this apartment 205?

¿Este es el apartamento 205?

=== PHRASE 5: Show order number ===

Here is your order number.

Aquí está tu número de orden.

=== PHRASE 6: Verify they ordered ===

Did you order food?

¿Ordenaste comida?

=== PHRASE 7: Request they come out ===

Can you come outside?

¿Puedes salir afuera?

=== PHRASE 8: Ask for gate code ===

What is the gate code?

¿Cuál es el código de la puerta?

# Resource 15: Rideshare Route Management & Navigation
**Level**: Intermedio
**Category**: Conductor
**Duration**: 5 minutes
**Phrases**: 19 essential phrases
**Focus**: Destination changes, route preferences, and navigation problems

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: DESTINATION VERIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Confirm address accuracy ===

Is this the correct address?

Is this the correct address?

¿Esta es la dirección correcta?

[Tip: Ask early if address looks unusual or far from passenger location]


=== PHRASE 2: Offer destination change ===

Do you want to change the destination?

Do you want to change the destination?

¿Quieres cambiar el destino?

[Tip: Use when passenger seems uncertain. Must change in app for payment]


=== PHRASE 3: Ask about additional stops ===

Do you need to make a stop?

Do you need to make a stop?

¿Necesitas hacer una parada?

[Tip: Clarify early. Some apps allow stops, some charge extra]


=== PHRASE 4: Confirm destination ===

Going to 123 Main Street, right?

Going to 123 Main Street, right?

Vamos a Main Street 123, ¿correcto?

[Tip: Quick confirmation. Catches app mistakes]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: BASIC DIRECTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 5: Announce right turn ===

Turn right

Turn right

Gira a la derecha

[Tip: Sometimes passenger gives manual directions. Acknowledge turns]


=== PHRASE 6: Announce left turn ===

Turn left

Turn left

Gira a la izquierda

[Tip: Repeat passenger's direction to confirm you understood]


=== PHRASE 7: Announce U-turn ===

Make a U-turn

Make a U-turn

Haz una vuelta en U

[Tip: Always check safety before U-turning]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: ROUTE PREFERENCES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Ask route preference ===

Which way do you prefer?

Which way do you prefer?

¿Qué ruta prefieres?

[Tip: Give passenger choice when multiple routes available]


=== PHRASE 9: Suggest highway route ===

Do you want to take the highway?

Do you want to take the highway?

¿Quieres tomar la autopista?

[Tip: Highway usually faster but passenger may prefer scenic route]


=== PHRASE 10: Ask about tolls ===

Do you mind tolls?

Do you mind tolls?

¿Te importan los peajes?

[Tip: Some passengers avoid tolls to save money. Ask before toll roads]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: GPS & NAVIGATION PROBLEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 11: Explain GPS signal loss ===

My GPS lost signal. One moment, please.

My GPS lost signal. One moment, please.

Mi GPS perdió señal. Un momento, por

[Tip: Stay calm. Passengers understand tech problems if you communicate]


=== PHRASE 12: Request passenger help ===

Can you help me with directions?

Can you help me with directions?

¿Me puedes ayudar con las direcciones?

[Tip: Not embarrassing to ask. Shows you value accurate arrival]


=== PHRASE 13: Report address discrepancy ===

The GPS shows a different address.

The GPS shows a different address.

El GPS muestra una dirección diferente.

[Tip: Alert passenger early to address mismatches]


=== PHRASE 14: Explain GPS restart ===

Let me restart the GPS.

Let me restart the GPS.

Déjame reiniciar el GPS.

[Tip: Simple fix that often works. Keeps passenger informed]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: ARRIVAL & ACCESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 15: Ask about entry point ===

Which entrance should I use?

Which entrance should I use?

¿Qué entrada debo usar?

[Tip: Hospitals, malls, and offices have multiple entrances]


=== PHRASE 16: Ask for gate code ===

Do I need a gate code?

Do I need a gate code?

¿Necesito un código de acceso?

[Tip: Ask before arriving at gated community]


=== PHRASE 17: Ask where to stop ===

Where should I park?

Where should I park?

¿Dónde debo estacionarme?

[Tip: Passenger knows best spot. Prevents having to move car]

SECTION 6: TRAFFIC & DELAYS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 18: Warn about delay ===

It might take 10 more minutes.

It might take 10 more minutes.

Puede tomar 10 minutos más.

[Tip: Manage expectations. Better to over-estimate than surprise passenger]


=== PHRASE 19: Offer route change ===

Should I take a different route?

Should I take a different route?

¿Debo tomar una ruta diferente?

[Tip: When stuck in traffic, give passenger option]

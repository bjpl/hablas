# Resource 5: Complex Delivery Situations - Intermediate
**Level**: Intermedio
**Category**: Repartidor
**Duration**: 5 minutes
**Phrases**: 9 advanced phrases
**Focus**: Handling special situations and difficult scenarios

SECTION 1: ORDER VERIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Request order confirmation ===

Can you double-check this order? The

Can you double-check this order? The

¿Puede verificar este pedido dos veces?

[Tip: Use when order looks incomplete or different than expected. Protects you from complaints]


=== PHRASE 2: Alert about allergies ===

The customer has allergies. Did you

The customer has allergies. Did you

El cliente tiene alergias. ¿Prepararon

[Tip: Important for customer safety. Shows you care about details]


=== PHRASE 3: Clarify restaurant statement ===

The restaurant says this is what was

The restaurant says this is what was

El restaurante dice que esto es lo que

[Tip: Use when customer questions the order. You're the messenger, not responsible]

SECTION 2: FOLLOWING INSTRUCTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 4: Reference delivery notes ===

Your delivery notes say [instruction],

Your delivery notes say [instruction],

Sus notas de entrega dicen

[Tip: Shows you read their instructions carefully. Replace [instruction] with their actual note]


=== PHRASE 5: Professional delivery greeting ===

Good evening, I have a food delivery for

Good evening, I have a food delivery for

Buenas noches, tengo una entrega de

[Tip: More formal greeting for office buildings or upscale locations]

SECTION 3: ACCESS PROBLEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 6: Report closed building ===

The building appears to be closed. Are

The building appears to be closed. Are

El edificio parece estar cerrado. ¿Aún

[Tip: Use for locked offices or businesses. Confirms they still want delivery]


=== PHRASE 7: Explain elevator issue ===

Hi, the elevator is out of order. I have

Hi, the elevator is out of order. I have

Hola, el ascensor no funciona. Tengo su

[Tip: Sets expectations before customer complains about delay. Honesty is best]

SECTION 4: HANDLING COMPLAINTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Show empathy ===

I completely understand your frustration.

I completely understand your frustration.

Entiendo completamente su frustración.

[Tip: De-escalates angry customers. Shows you care even when it's not your fault]


=== PHRASE 9: Assert professional boundaries ===

I understand, but I need to complete this

I understand, but I need to complete this

Entiendo, pero necesito completar esta

[Tip: Use when customer is upset but you must finish delivery to get paid. Stay polite but firm]

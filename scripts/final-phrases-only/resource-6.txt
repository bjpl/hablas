# Resource 6: Restaurant Pickup & Delivery Essentials
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Repartidor
**Duration**: 4 minutes
**Phrases**: 17 essential phrases
**Focus**: Restaurant interactions and complete delivery cycle

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: GREETING RESTAURANT STAFF
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Polite greeting ===

Hello! How are you?

Hello! How are you?

¡Hola! ¿Cómo estás?

[Tip: Start friendly. Good relationships with restaurant staff = faster service]


=== PHRASE 2: Positive response ===

Good, thank you!

Good, thank you!

Bien, ¡gracias!

[Tip: Even if you're having a bad day, be positive with restaurant staff]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: IDENTIFYING YOUR ORDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 3: State customer name ===

Order for [customer name].

Order for [customer name].

Orden para [nombre del cliente]

[Tip: Say the customer's name clearly. Replace [customer name] with actual name from app]


=== PHRASE 4: Give order number ===

Order number [order number].

Order number [order number].

Número de orden [número de orden]

[Tip: Use when restaurant has many orders waiting. Be ready to show your phone]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: CHECKING ORDER STATUS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 5: Ask if ready ===

Is it ready?

Is it ready?

¿Está lista?

[Tip: Use after you've given the order info. Don't ask this immediately when you walk in]


=== PHRASE 6: Request verification ===

Can you check the order?

Can you check the order?

¿Puede verificar la orden?

[Tip: Use when you've been waiting a while or if they can't find it]


=== PHRASE 7: Ask about wait time ===

How much longer?

How much longer?

¿Cuánto tiempo más?

[Tip: Be polite. Restaurant staff appreciate patience]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: REQUESTING ITEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Request drinks ===

I need the drinks too.

I need the drinks too.

Necesito las bebidas también

[Tip: Check before leaving! Drinks are often separate from food]


=== PHRASE 9: Request extras ===

Can I have napkins and utensils?

Can I have napkins and utensils?

¿Me puede dar servilletas y cubiertos?

[Tip: Many restaurants forget these. Customers complain if missing]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: HANDLING PROBLEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 10: Report issue to support ===

I need to contact support.

I need to contact support.

Necesito contactar a soporte

[Tip: Say this to restaurant if there's a problem you can't solve. Then actually call support]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 6: DELIVERING TO CUSTOMER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 11: Announce arrival ===

Hi! I have your delivery.

Hi! I have your delivery.

¡Hola! Tengo tu entrega.

[Tip: Classic greeting. Works every time]


=== PHRASE 12: Confirm identity ===

Are you [customer name]?

Are you [customer name]?

¿Eres [nombre del cliente]?

[Tip: Always verify before handing over food]


=== PHRASE 13: Request verification ===

Please check everything.

Please check everything.

Por favor revise todo.

[Tip: Protects you if customer later claims something is missing]


=== PHRASE 14: Closing phrase ===

Enjoy your meal!

Enjoy your meal!

¡Disfruta tu comida!

[Tip: Friendly way to end the delivery. Increases tips]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 7: ACCESS ISSUES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 15: Request customer comes out ===

Can you come outside?

Can you come outside?

¿Puedes salir?

[Tip: Use when you can't access building or find apartment]


=== PHRASE 16: Request building access ===

Can you let me in?

Can you let me in?

¿Me puedes dejar entrar?

[Tip: For locked building doors. Also works: "Can you buzz me in?"]

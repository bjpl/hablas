# Resource 19: Navigation & Address Verification
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: All (Conductor & Repartidor)
**Duration**: 3 minutes
**Phrases**: 13 essential phrases
**Focus**: Finding locations and confirming addresses

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: ADDRESS VERIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Confirm building number ===

Is it number 245?

Is it number 245?

¿Es el número 245?

[Tip: Replace with actual number. Confirms you have correct building]


=== PHRASE 2: Confirm street name ===

Is this Main Street?

Is this Main Street?

¿Es esta la calle Main?

[Tip: Use when street signs are unclear or missing]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: REQUESTING DIRECTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 3: Ask how to get there ===

How do I get there?

How do I get there?

¿Cómo llego ahí?

[Tip: Direct question when GPS fails]


=== PHRASE 4: Ask where to turn ===

Where do I turn?

Where do I turn?

¿Dónde giro?

[Tip: Quick clarification needed at intersections]


=== PHRASE 5: Confirm you're going right way ===

Am I going the right way?

Am I going the right way?

¿Voy por el camino correcto?

[Tip: Shows humility. Customers/passengers appreciate honesty]


=== PHRASE 6: Ask which side ===

Which side of the street?

Which side of the street?

¿Qué lado de la calle?

[Tip: Prevents parking on wrong side]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: ADDRESS PROBLEMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 7: Report app error ===

The address is wrong in the app

The address is wrong in the app

La dirección está mal en la app

[Tip: Stay calm. Explain issue without blaming customer]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: ROUTE PREFERENCES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Offer route alternative ===

Do you prefer a different route?

Do you prefer a different route?

¿Prefieres una ruta diferente?

[Tip: Gives customer/passenger choice. Shows flexibility]


=== PHRASE 9: Ask about highway ===

Should I take the highway?

Should I take the highway?

¿Debería tomar la autopista?

[Tip: Some prefer scenic routes or avoiding tolls]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: ARRIVAL & PARKING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 10: Ask which corner ===

Which corner?

Which corner?

¿Qué esquina?

[Tip: Intersections have 4 corners. Clarify exact spot]


=== PHRASE 11: Ask where to stop ===

Where should I stop?

Where should I stop?

¿Dónde debería parar?

[Tip: Let customer/passenger choose best spot]


=== PHRASE 12: Ask about parking ===

Can I park here?

Can I park here?

¿Puedo estacionarme aquí?

[Tip: Checks legality and convenience]


=== PHRASE 13: Offer to get closer ===

I can pull up closer

I can pull up closer

Puedo acercarme más

[Tip: Shows helpfulness, especially in rain or with luggage]

NAVIGATION TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When lost or confused:
1. Stay calm
2. Pull over safely
3. Ask for help

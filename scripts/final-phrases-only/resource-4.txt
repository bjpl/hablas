# Resource 4: Essential Delivery Phrases - Variation 4
**Level**: Básico
**Category**: Repartidor
**Duration**: 4 minutes
**Phrases**: 15 essential phrases
**Focus**: Common delivery scenarios and problem-solving

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1: ARRIVAL & GREETING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 1: Quick greeting ===

Hi! Delivery for you.

Hi! Delivery for you.

¡Hola! Entrega para ti.

[Tip: Short and efficient for busy deliveries]


=== PHRASE 2: Evening delivery greeting ===

Good evening! I have your food.

Good evening! I have your food.

Buenas noches! Tengo tu comida.

[Tip: Use after 5pm. "Good evening" sounds more professional than just "Hi"]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 2: CONFIRMING DETAILS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 3: Verify customer name ===

Are you Sarah?

Are you Sarah?

¿Eres Sarah?

[Tip: Always confirm name before handing over food]


=== PHRASE 4: Verify apartment number ===

Is this apartment 205?

Is this apartment 205?

¿Este es el apartamento 205?

[Tip: Use when apartment numbers are confusing or hard to see]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 3: DELIVERING THE ORDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 5: Hand over the order ===

Here you go!

Here you go!

¡Aquí tienes!

[Tip: Simple and friendly. Works for any delivery]


=== PHRASE 6: Confirm contents ===

Everything is in the bag.

Everything is in the bag.

Todo está en la bolsa.

[Tip: Reassures customer that nothing is missing]


=== PHRASE 7: Request app confirmation ===

Can you confirm on my phone?

Can you confirm on my phone?

¿Puedes confirmar en mi teléfono?

[Tip: Needed for some orders. Hand them your phone with the confirmation screen ready]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 4: NAVIGATION & ACCESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 8: Request help finding location ===

Can you help me find you?

Can you help me find you?

¿Puedes ayudarme a encontrarte?

[Tip: Use when GPS isn't accurate or building layout is confusing]


=== PHRASE 9: Confirm visual contact ===

Do you see me?

Do you see me?

¿Me ves?

[Tip: Helpful when you're outside but customer can't locate you]


=== PHRASE 10: Request building access ===

Can you buzz me in?

Can you buzz me in?

¿Puedes abrirme la puerta?

[Tip: "Buzz in" means open the locked building door remotely]


=== PHRASE 11: Ask for floor number ===

Which floor?

Which floor?

¿Qué piso?

[Tip: Quick question when customer says to come up but doesn't specify floor]


=== PHRASE 12: Request outdoor meetup ===

Can you meet me outside?

Can you meet me outside?

¿Puedes encontrarme afuera?

[Tip: Use when you can't access building or to save time]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 5: SPECIAL SITUATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

=== PHRASE 13: Warning about drinks ===

Be careful, there are drinks.

Be careful, there are drinks.

Ten cuidado, hay bebidas.

[Tip: Prevents spills. Customer appreciates the heads-up]


=== PHRASE 14: Explain your role ===

I just pick up and deliver.

I just pick up and deliver.

Solo recojo y entrego.

[Tip: Use when customer complains about missing items or wrong order. You're not responsible for restaurant mistakes]


=== PHRASE 15: Explain communication attempt ===

I tried calling you.

I tried calling you.

Intenté llamarte.

[Tip: Shows you made effort to reach them. Good for difficult deliveries]

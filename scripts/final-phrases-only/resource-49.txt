================================================================================
RESOURCE 49: PAYMENT DISPUTES
================================================================================
Category: Emergency & Safety - Financial Conflict Resolution
Difficulty: Intermediate
Context: Professional handling of fare disputes and payment issues
Legal Note: NEVER accept cash payments outside the app. All payments must be
processed through platform for tax, insurance, and legal protection.
================================================================================

SECTION 1: KEY TERMINOLOGY - PAYMENT VOCABULARY
================================================================================

1. Payment declined
   Context: Credit card or payment method was rejected
   Pago rechazado / declinado

2. Cash payment
   Context: Physical currency (NOT allowed in most rideshare)
   Pago en efectivo

3. Charge dispute
   Context: Passenger contests amount charged
   Disputa de cargo

4. Processing issue
   Context: Technical problem with payment system
   Problema de procesamiento

5. Tip / Gratuity
   Context: Optional extra payment for good service
   Propina

================================================================================
SECTION 2: PAYMENT PROCESSING ISSUES - TECHNICAL PROBLEMS
================================================================================

6. The app is showing a payment processing issue, but this doesn't affect the trip. The platform will reach out to you about updating your payment method.

   Context: Payment declined notification during trip
   When to use: App alerts you of payment issue
   Professional approach: Complete trip normally, platform handles collection
   Driver protection: You still get paid, platform pursues collection

   La aplicación está mostrando un problema de procesamiento de pago, pero esto no afecta el viaje. La plataforma se comunicará con usted sobre actualizar su método de pago.

7. I appreciate the offer, but all payments must go through the app. This protects both of us and ensures everything is documented. You can add a new payment method in the app settings.

   Context: Passenger offers cash due to payment issue
   When to use: Declining cash payment (REQUIRED for legal protection)
   Legal protection: Off-app payments void insurance and create tax issues
   Safety: No documentation = no protection if dispute arises

   Agradezco la oferta, pero todos los pagos deben hacerse a través de la aplicación. Esto nos protege a ambos y asegura que todo quede documentado. Puede agregar un nuevo método de pago en la configuración de la aplicación.

SECTION 3: FARE AMOUNT DISPUTES - PRICE COMPLAINTS
================================================================================

8. I understand the price seems high. The fare is calculated by the app based on distance, time, and current demand. I don't set the price. If you believe there's an error, you can dispute it through app support and they'll review it.

   Context: Passenger complains fare is too expensive
   When to use: Price objection during or after trip
   Key points: You don't control pricing, explain factors, offer dispute process
   De-escalation: Acknowledge concern, explain system, provide solution path

   Entiendo que el precio parece alto. La tarifa es calculada por la aplicación según distancia, tiempo y demanda actual. Yo no establezco el precio. Si cree que hay un error, puede disputarlo a través del soporte de la aplicación y ellos lo revisarán.

9. Just so you know, there's surge pricing right now due to high demand. The app should have shown you the increased fare when you booked.

   Context: Explaining surge/dynamic pricing
   When to use: Passenger didn't notice surge multiplier when booking
   Education: Help them understand surge pricing for future bookings

   Para que sepa, hay tarifa de demanda alta ahora mismo debido a alta demanda. La aplicación debería haberle mostrado la tarifa aumentada cuando reservó.

10. I see this is a longer trip. The fare will be based on time and distance, approximately [range] based on the route.

    Context: Setting expectations for long-distance fare
    When to use: At pickup for trips over 30 minutes
    Transparency: Prevents surprise at destination

    Veo que este es un viaje más largo. La tarifa se basará en tiempo y distancia, aproximadamente [rango] según la ruta.

SECTION 4: ROUTE DISPUTES - "YOU TOOK THE LONG WAY"

11. I'm following the app's navigation, which chooses the fastest route based on current traffic. If you prefer a specific route, please let me know.

    Context: Defending route choice while offering passenger input
    When to use: Passenger questions your routing
    GPS evidence: App tracks exact route taken - you're protected
    Customer service: Offer to use their preferred route if practical

    Estoy siguiendo la navegación de la aplicación, que elige la ruta más rápida según el tráfico actual. Si prefiere una ruta específica, por favor avíseme.

PAYMENT DISPUTE PROTOCOLS
================================================================================

HANDLING PRICE COMPLAINTS:
1. Never apologize for the price (you didn't set it)
2. Explain how algorithmic pricing works
3. Reference distance, time, and demand factors
4. Offer platform dispute process
5. Never offer to refund or adjust yourself
6. Document conversation in trip notes

WHEN PAYMENT IS DECLINED:
1. Complete the trip normally
2. Platform will pursue payment collection
3. You will still receive your fare
4. Do NOT accept alternative payment
5. If passenger refuses to pay: Contact platform support immediately

CASH PAYMENT POLICY:
❌ NEVER ACCEPT CASH PAYMENTS ❌

Why this rule protects you:
- Insurance only covers on-platform transactions
- No record = no proof of payment if dispute arises
- Creates unreported income / tax issues
- Passenger could claim they paid more than they did
- Could be accused of theft if passenger reports "forced cash payment"

If passenger insists on cash:
1. Politely decline (use phrase #7)
2. Direct to app payment update process
3. If they cannot pay through app: End trip, let platform handle
4. Never complete trip for cash payment

DOCUMENTATION FOR DISPUTES:
After any payment dispute:
1. Add detailed trip notes immediately
2. Include: passenger's complaint, your explanation, outcome
3. Screenshot final fare breakdown
4. If serious dispute: Request platform review before passenger can file claim
5. Keep records for at least 6 months for tax/legal purposes

SURGE PRICING COMPLAINTS:
- Surge is disclosed in app before booking
- Screenshot shows passenger accepted surge price
- You cannot control or change surge multiplier
- Platform support handles all surge disputes
- Be empathetic but firm: pricing is not negotiable

TIPS AND SERVICE QUALITY:
- Tips are optional, never required
- Never pressure passengers for tips
- Quality service increases tip likelihood
- Most tips are added post-trip in app
- Platform cannot force tips for complaints

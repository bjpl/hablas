COMMUNICATION EXCELLENCE TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ TONE MATTERS:
- Warm but not overly familiar
- Confident but not arrogant
- Friendly but professional
- Helpful but maintain boundaries

✓ BODY LANGUAGE (for rideshare):
- Smile when appropriate
- Make brief eye contact
- Open posture
- Calm movements

✓ WRITTEN COMMUNICATION (texts/app):
- Use proper grammar and spelling
- Keep messages brief and clear
- Be prompt in responding
- Stay professional always

✓ VOICE TONE:
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Adjust volume to environment
- Stay calm under stress
- Use positive inflection

Remember: How you communicate is as important as what you communicate. Professional communication builds trust and earns tips.

# Lost Items Management - Audio Script
## Artículos Perdidos - Manejo Profesional

**Duration**: 7 minutes | **Level**: Intermediate | **Category**: All Workers

---

## PROTECT YOURSELF

ALWAYS report found items through platform.
NEVER contact customer directly outside app.
NEVER meet in private places to return items.

Platform protects you when you follow procedures.

---

## Phrase 1: Item Left Behind

### English:
"You left something in the car"
"You left something in the car"

### Spanish:
Dejó algo en el carro.

Say IMMEDIATELY if you see passenger still visible.

---

## Phrase 2: I Found Your Item

### English:
"I found your phone"
"I found your phone"

### Spanish:
Encontré su teléfono.

When reporting through platform. Change "phone" to: wallet, keys, etc.

---

## Phrase 3: Reported Through App

### English:
"I've reported it through the app"
"I've reported it through the app"

### Spanish:
Lo he reportado a través de la aplicación.

Explain correct process.

---

## Phrase 4: Your Item is Safe

### English:
"Your item is safe"
"Your item is safe"

### Spanish:
Su artículo está seguro.

Reassure worried customer.

---

## Phrase 5: Describe What You Lost

### English:
"Can you describe what you lost?"
"Can you describe what you lost?"

### Spanish:
¿Puede describir lo que perdió?

Verify correct owner before returning.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP: Found Item

Step 1 - If passenger still visible:
"Excuse me! You left something in the car!"

Step 2 - If discovered later:
"I found an item in my vehicle after your ride."

Step 3 - Take photo and report:
"I'm taking a photo for documentation and reporting it to the platform."

Step 4 - Communicate through app:
"The platform will connect us to arrange the return."

Step 5 - Safe return location:
"Let's meet at a public location to return your item."

---

## High-Value Items

Phone:
"I found your phone. It's been turned off and stored safely."

Wallet:
"I found your wallet. I haven't opened it. Everything is secure."

Keys:
"I have your keys. Where would you like to meet to get them back?"

---

## Verify Ownership

Ask to describe:
"Can you describe the item to verify it's yours?"

Check ID:
"Can I see your ID to confirm it matches the app?"

---

## Safe Return Locations

Suggest public place:
"Can we meet at the Starbucks / police station / grocery store?"

Best option:
"I can meet you at the police station parking lot. It's safe for both of us."

NEVER accept:
"I'm not comfortable meeting at a private residence."

---

## Return Fee

Explain fee:
"The platform arranged a return fee of $[amount] for the trip to return your item."

If asking about compensation:
"A return fee covers my time and gas. The platform will process it."

---

## IF FALSELY ACCUSED

First response:
"I've checked my vehicle thoroughly and don't have your item."

Report to platform:
"I'm reporting this to the platform. They have GPS and timing records of all passengers after you."

Offer evidence:
"I can provide photos showing my vehicle is empty."

---

## ESSENTIAL VOCABULARY

Lost item = Artículo perdido
Found = Encontrado
Return = Devolver
Valuable = Valioso
Public place = Lugar público
Documentation = Documentación

---

## CRITICAL PRACTICE

Immediate discovery:
1. "You left something in the car!"
2. "Your phone / wallet / keys"

Later discovery:
1. "I found your [item]"
2. "I've reported it through the app"
3. "Your item is safe"
4. "Can you describe what you lost?"

---

## REMEMBER

1. ALWAYS report through platform system
2. NEVER contact customers outside app
3. ALWAYS take photos of found items
4. ONLY meet in public places during day
5. Verify identity before returning
6. Return fee is appropriate and normal

Following these steps protects you legally and keeps everything professional.

---

**Voice Notes**:
- Professional, helpful tone
- Emphasize platform procedures
- Safety in return logistics
- Clear documentation protocols

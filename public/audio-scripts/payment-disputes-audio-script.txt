# Payment Disputes - Audio Script
## Disputas de Pago

**Duration**: 8 minutes | **Level**: Intermediate | **Category**: All Workers

---

## PROTECT YOURSELF

NEVER accept payments outside app. Platform guarantees your payment even if customer doesn't pay.

All disputes must be handled through platform.

---

## Phrase 1: Price Set By App

### English:
"The price is set by the app"
"The price is set by the app"

### Spanish:
El precio lo establece la aplicación.

When customer complains about price. Emphasize YOU don't control it.

---

## Phrase 2: Cannot Change Fare

### English:
"I cannot change the fare"
"I cannot change the fare"

### Spanish:
No puedo cambiar la tarifa.

When customer asks for discount or reduction.

---

## Phrase 3: Dispute Through Support

### English:
"You can dispute this through app support"
"You can dispute this through app support"

### Spanish:
Puede disputar esto a través del soporte de la aplicación.

Redirect price complaints to platform. This ends most disputes.

---

## Phrase 4: All Payments Through App

### English:
"All payments must go through the app"
"All payments must go through the app"

### Spanish:
Todos los pagos deben hacerse a través de la aplicación.

If customer offers cash payment.

---

## Phrase 5: Protects Both of Us

### English:
"This protects both of us"
"This protects both of us"

### Spanish:
Esto nos protege a ambos.

Explain WHY everything must be through app.

---

## Fare Dispute Steps

Step 1 - Show empathy:
"I understand the price seems high."

Step 2 - Explain system:
"The fare is calculated by the app based on distance, time, and demand."

Step 3 - Show evidence:
"Here's the fare breakdown on the screen."

Step 4 - Redirect:
"If you believe there's an error, you can dispute it through app support."

---

## Card Declined

To customer:
"The app is showing a payment processing issue. The platform will contact you about updating your payment method."

Reassure:
"This doesn't affect the trip. You still get to your destination."

NEVER say: "Can you pay cash instead?"
Let platform handle payment problems.

---

## Surge Pricing

Explain:
"There's surge pricing right now due to high demand. The app showed you the increased fare when you booked."

If they say didn't see:
"The app always shows the fare before you confirm. If you believe there's an error, app support can review it."

---

## Cash Payment Offer

Refuse politely:
"I appreciate the offer, but all payments must go through the app. This protects both of us."

Explain why:
"Off-app payments remove our insurance coverage and documentation."

---

## Tips (Propinas)

Subtle and professional:
"If you were satisfied with the service, I appreciate any consideration. Thank you!"

NEVER SAY:
- "Tips are expected"
- "Most people tip"
- "You forgot to tip"

If customer asks:
"Tips are appreciated but not required. It's entirely up to you."

---

## Fraud & Extortion

Customer threatens false report:
"All trip details are recorded by the platform including GPS, time, and communication. I'll submit a report about this conversation."

Don't give in to blackmail:
"I cannot provide refunds or change fares. Only the platform can do that."

---

## CRITICAL PHRASES

1. "The price is set by the app"
2. "I cannot change the fare"
3. "You can dispute through app support"
4. "All payments must go through the app"
5. "This protects both of us"

---

## REMEMBER

1. NEVER accept off-app payments - lose all protection
2. You don't control prices - app does
3. Redirect ALL disputes to app support
4. Mention tips subtly or not at all
5. Document threats or fraud attempts
6. Platform guarantees your payment

---

**Voice Notes**:
- Professional, boundary-setting
- Firm but polite
- Emphasize platform protection
- Clear refusal of off-app payments

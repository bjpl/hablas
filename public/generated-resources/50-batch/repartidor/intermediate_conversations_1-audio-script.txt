## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR AUDIO TEAM

### VOICE REQUIREMENTS:
- **Spanish Narrator**: Male or female, 30-45 years old, neutral Latin American accent (Mexican/Colombian preferred), warm and encouraging tone
- **English Speaker**: Native speaker (American accent), clear articulation, patient teaching tone

### AUDIO SPECIFICATIONS:
- **Format**: MP3, 128 kbps minimum
- **Background**: Subtle ambient music during intro/outro only (low volume)
- **Effects**: None during teaching sections - crystal clear voice only
- **Volume**: Normalize to -3dB, consistent levels throughout

### PACING:
- English phrases at 80% speed: Use time-stretching, NOT pitch-shifting
- Pauses must be EXACT - critical for learning effectiveness
- Spanish sections: Natural conversational pace

### QUALITY CHECKS:
- ✅ All phonetic pronunciations accurate
- ✅ Pause durations precise (3s and 2s)
- ✅ No background noise during teaching
- ✅ Clear distinction between first and repeat English phrases
- ✅ Consistent energy throughout (no fade in enthusiasm)

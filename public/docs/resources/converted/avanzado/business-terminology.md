## Cultural Notes

### Independent Contractor Status

You are self-employed in US system - responsible for your own taxes, insurance, and expenses

**Colombian Comparison:** Very different from Colombian employee protections; no benefits from platforms

### Tax Obligations

Must file quarterly estimated taxes; keep detailed records of income and expenses

**Colombian Comparison:** More individual responsibility than Colombian employment tax withholding

### Platform Algorithms

Ratings, acceptance rates, and completion rates affect how many jobs you receive

**Colombian Comparison:** Performance metrics directly impact earning opportunity

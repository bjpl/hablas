### Overall Visual Guide
```
Format: Mobile Portrait Guide (1080x1920px)
Total Pages: 10
Style: Clean, modern, high-contrast
Primary Colors:
  - Uber: #000000 (Black)
  - Rappi: #FF441F (Orange)

  - Success: #00C853

  - Info: #1976D2
```
